Welcome to CredBud.
Take a Useful Advice.

Tracking expenses for students is a real HASSLE. The students are busy with their academics making it difficult for them to keep track on their daily expenditure. CredBud thus aims to reduce this inconvenience by providing an appealing and interactive platform which will help the students to record their daily as well as the monthly expenses and analyze their personal expenses visually using graphs.
